Methodology / Data Cutoff

How to read the model

This portfolio uses public information and the Excel model output. SOTP is the primary valuation method; DCF is kept as a cross-check, not the main target-price engine.

Most important KPI to monitor

BHX RPSM is the cleanest check. If store count rises but RPSM fails to recover, revenue growth is less valuable because new-store dilution may be lasting.

Why SOTP over DCF

MWG mixes mature ICT/CE retail, grocery expansion, and smaller optionality chains. SOTP separates those profiles, while DCF mainly tests whether the target still makes sense under cash-flow and discount-rate assumptions.
